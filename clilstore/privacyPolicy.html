Clilstore privacy policy

Usernames, e-mail addresses and any other personal data or personal statistical data will be used only for the purpose of the Clilstore project. They will not be released to outside parties.

Passwords are stored only in securely encrypted format. There is no way in which they can be decrypted by project staff or anyone else.

Like any other login facility on the Internet, Clilstore uses cookies, but these contain only a code, no personal information.
