Multidict will work better if you enable cookies

Multidict uses a cookie to remember your choice of target language and dictionary - so that you don’t need to keep reselecting these every time! You seem to have cookies disabled in your browser, but it is very easy to reenable them.

The way to do this depends on which browser you are using - Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, Opera or whatever - but you’ll find instructions on any of the following pages:

  • from the University of Delaware
  • from AboutCookies.org
  • from Google
(By the way, Multidict does not require “third party cookies”, just normal cookies)
