Wordlink

Wordlink is a WWW based facility which links arbitrary webpages automatically, word by word with online dictionaries. It was developed by Caoimhín Ó Donnaíle at Sabhal Mòr Ostaig as part of the European funded POOLS-T project (2008-2010), and development is being continued as part of the European funded TOOLS project (2012-2014).

It works in conjunction with Multidict, a multiple dictionary lookup facility, and it is the basis of Clilstore, a store of copyleft content and language integrated teaching materials.

I would be very glad indeed to receive comments or suggestions on this facility - Simply send them by e-mail (in any language) to caoimhin@smo.uhi.ac.uk. If you have suggestions as to other online dictionaries which you think would be worth adding to the system, I would be very happy to consider them.
